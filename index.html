programming /ˈprōˌgramiNG/ mass noun
  1. the writing of a computer program
  2. the process of preparing an instructional program
  3. the planning, scheduling, or performing of a program

hack /hak/ intransitive verb
  1. to write computer programs for enjoyment
  2. to gain unauthorized access to a computer
